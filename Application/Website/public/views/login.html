Welcome to Aegis

Aegis is a smart home thermostat that adjusts your house's temperature based on several factors:

  • Electricity provider rates
  • Outside temperature
  • Solar panel production
  • Your personal preferences

In addition, Aegis includes dongles that can be plugged into your appliances to monitor their energy consumption.
By gathering this information, Aegis optimizes your energy consumption and helps you save money.
